## Overview

Connected ("the app") is a trivia chain game developed by Cthalepoú LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Display name** — chosen by you during account creation, shown on leaderboards
- **Email address** — used for account authentication and communication
- **User ID** — a unique identifier assigned to your account by our authentication system

### Information Collected Automatically
- **App interactions** — quiz scores, completion times, and session data used for leaderboards and personal bests
- **Crash logs and performance data** — used to identify and fix technical issues

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Create and manage your Connected account
- Display your scores on leaderboards
- Save your personal best scores and quiz history
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Respond to support requests

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Share your data with third parties except as described below

## Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

---

## Children's Privacy

Connected is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at connectedquiz@gmail.com.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data (via the Profile tab in Settings)
- **Delete** your account and all associated data (via the Profile tab in Settings)
- **Contact us** with any privacy concerns at connectedquiz@gmail.com

---

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and encrypted local storage to protect your information.
