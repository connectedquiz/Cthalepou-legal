## How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Connected account directly from within the app at any time. No need to contact us.

### Steps to Delete Your Account

1. Open the **Connected** app
2. Tap the **hamburger menu** (≡) in the top-right corner
3. Tap **Profile** tab
4. Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile tab
5. Tap **Delete Account**
6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your display name and email address
- Your quiz history and scores
- Your leaderboard entries
- Your subscription and purchase records
- All locally cached app data

---

## What Cannot Be Recovered

**Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.** Once deleted:

- Your account cannot be restored
- Your scores and quiz history cannot be recovered
- Your leaderboard rankings will be removed
- Any active subscriptions will not be refunded — please cancel subscriptions through the App Store or Google Play before deleting your account

---

## Cancel Subscriptions Before Deleting

Before deleting your account, we recommend cancelling any active Connected subscriptions to avoid future charges:

- **iOS:** Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Connected → Cancel
- **Android:** Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Connected → Cancel
